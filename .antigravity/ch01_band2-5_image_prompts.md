## Global Style Anchors

### DK Encyclopedia Style (Bands 2-3)
**Always include:** "DK Encyclopedia-style educational illustration, labeled diagram, clean white or cream background, precise annotations with thin leader lines, educational callout boxes, cross-section cutaway view, flat lighting, scientific illustration quality, 640x640 square format, no text except labels"
**Always exclude:** "cartoon, anime, 3D render, photorealistic photo, watercolor, painterly, blurry, low detail, stock photo"
**Color palette:** Clean whites, warm creams, precise earth tones for subjects, thin black outlines, blue/amber/green accent colors for labels matching brand palette (Laterite #C4622D, Amber #E8A838, Forest #2C6E49)

### Documentary Style (Bands 4-5)
**Always include:** "Scholarly documentary illustration, museum-catalog quality, archival manuscript style, muted earth tones, aged parchment texture, precise pen-and-ink linework with watercolor wash, academic annotation style, 640x640 square format"
**Always exclude:** "cartoon, anime, 3D render, childish, bright saturated colors, modern graphic design, clip art"
**Color palette:** Sepia, umber, muted ochre, aged ivory, iron gall ink brown, subtle gold leaf accents
