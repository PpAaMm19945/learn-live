
### Summary & Review

**Key Takeaways:**

1.  **God is the Author of History:** All of history, including African history, unfolds under the sovereign plan of God, framed by the biblical narrative of Creation, Fall, and Redemption.
2.  **Africa's Biblical Roots:** The descendants of Ham (Cush, Mizraim, and Phut) were foundational to the post-Babel world, establishing great civilizations in Africa.
3.  **The "Curse of Ham" is a Myth:** The biblical curse was on Canaan, not Ham, and had nothing to do with race. The myth was a later invention to justify slavery.
4.  **Scripture is our Foundation:** We must evaluate all historical theories, including those about African origins, through the lens of God's inerrant Word.
5.  **God's Grace and Judgment:** God's common grace allows nations to flourish, but pride and idolatry eventually lead to judgment.
