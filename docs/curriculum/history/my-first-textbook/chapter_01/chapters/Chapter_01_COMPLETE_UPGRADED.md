**Chapter Summary (≤100 words):** This chapter establishes a biblical framework for understanding African history, beginning with God's creation and the subsequent fall of man. It traces the lineage of African peoples to Noah's son Ham, refutes the "Curse of Ham" myth, and presents a narrative of God's sovereign plan for the continent, emphasizing dignity, purpose, and redemption.

**Key Terms:** sovereignty, covenant, common grace, saving grace, curse, mishpat, tzedakah.
