## Critical Thinking Exercises

1.  **Empire and Identity (150-250 words):** The Napatan kings conquered Egypt and ruled as the 25th Dynasty. In your view, were they "Egyptian" or "Nubian" rulers? How did they use elements of both cultures to legitimize their authority?
2.  **Technology and Society (150-250 words):** Discuss the arguments for and against Meroitic iron production being a "liberating" technology. How do societies today grapple with the dual-use nature of powerful new technologies?
3.  **Faith and Power (150-250 words):** Analyze the complex relationship between King Ezana's military conquest of Meroe and his conversion to Christianity. Is it possible for a political leader's faith to be genuine even when their actions seem to contradict the teachings of that faith? Use examples from the text to support your answer.
4.  **Memory vs. History (150-250 words):** Explain the difference between "cultural memory" (like the Queen of Sheba narrative) and "archaeological history." Why are both important for understanding a people's identity?
